# Phase 16 — Stewardship + long-term governance

**Status:** ⚪ Planned.

## Goal

Outlive the founder. Valenx as an institution, not a project.

## Capability inventory

- Foundation charter (Linux Foundation, NumFOCUS, or independent
  501(c)(3)).
- Documented BDFL-to-steering-committee succession plan.
- Trademark + domain held by the foundation, not an individual.
- Reproducibility guarantees in writing: any `.valenx` project ships
  with everything needed to re-run its results for at least 10 years.
- LTS release cadence of 18 months, each supported 24 months.
- Public roadmap retrospective every year.

## Acceptance checklist

- [ ] Legal entity exists and holds assets.
- [ ] ≥ 3 maintainers not employed by the same organisation.
- [ ] 5-year financial runway secured by diversified sponsors.
- [ ] First LTS declared and supported through two minor upgrades.

## End state

At the end of Phase 16, Valenx is a permanent fixture of the
open-source simulation landscape: free to use, free to modify, free
to fork, stewarded by a community. The founder's direct involvement
may or may not continue, but the project no longer depends on it.
